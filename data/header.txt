The first field is Request ID which is not a part of the sequence. Sequence in fact stzarts from the second position. The last character is always a $ which is not a part of sequence. It is just a delimiter!
The meaning of numbers, however are written in the following
0: UNKNOWN, 
1: SYSTEMCALL, 
2: USERMODE, 
3: BLOCKED_IO, 
4: BLOCKED_CPU, 
5: BLOCKED_WAITKERNEL,
6: BLOCKED_WAITPROCESS, 
7: BLOCKED_FOR_FUTEX, 
8: BLOCKED_CPU_FUTEX, 
9: TRASH
